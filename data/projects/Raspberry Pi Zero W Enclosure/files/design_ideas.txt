## Design Ideas
*   **Form Factor:** Compact, minimalist design. Consider a 3D-printed case.
*   **Mounting:** Secure mounting for the Pi Zero W, speaker, and microphone.
*   **Ventilation:** Adequate ventilation to prevent overheating.

## Technical Specifications
*   **Microphone:** USB microphone (e.g., Adafruit I2S Mic).  Consider placement for optimal audio capture.
*   **Speaker:** Small, amplified speaker (e.g., PAM8403).  Placement for clear audio output.
*   **Power:** USB power supply.
*   **Connectivity:** USB for microphone and speaker, GPIO pins for any additional sensors or controls.

## Materials
*   3D Printing Filament (PLA recommended)
*   USB Microphone
*   Amplified Speaker
*   USB Power Supply
